Pete "The Jukester" Sheridan

Blues Musician, Author

The late Peter Sheridan, better known in the blues circuit as "The Jukester," was a harp player, guitarist and lead singer in numerous bands in the greater State College area and Los Angeles for decades. He authored several definitive texts on harmonica history, blues tone, rare guitars and amplifiers. Pete shared the stage with Buddy Guy, Bo Diddley, Clarence "Gatemoth" Brown, amoung many others. This page is dedicated to preserving his work and memory.
